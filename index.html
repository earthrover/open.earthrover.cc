OpenER

Welcome to Earth Rover's OpenER project!

This project will centralise and develop the ROS modules required for the open-source rover we are building that uses low-cost, off-the-shelf components. We are looking to work closely with the open-source ROS and robotics community. Code will be developed in line with ROS-I standards.

Users will access 3D models, the parts list, ROS packages and documentation in one place and build the inexpensive rover themselves. Control, planning and localisation will be ruggedised for farm use.

If you're a research institution or a company, get in touch if you want to support development of this rover - instead of buying expensive proprietary rovers or existing research-only rovers.

About
Earth Rover is a UK start-up aiming to provide the first robotic agriculture system that is genuinely viable for farmers. We are driven by the need to bridge the gap between today’s farming methods and the autonomous farm of the future. We call our approach the “pragmatic transition”: combining best-in-class technologies with existing equipment to deliver working applications — such as scouting, weeding and selective harvesting — that help farmers improve productivity.
